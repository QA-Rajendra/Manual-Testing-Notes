#### 1Ô∏è‚É£ What is SDLC?

#### 

**Answer:**

*   **Definition:** SDLC (Software Development Life Cycle) is a structured process for **designing, developing, and maintaining software**.
    
*   **Purpose:** Ensures software is **high-quality, error-free, and meets client requirements**.
    
*   **Memory Tip:** Think of SDLC as a **roadmap for software** ‚Äî like planning a journey before starting. üõ£Ô∏è
    
*   **Example:** Building WhatsApp follows SDLC: requirements ‚Üí design ‚Üí coding ‚Üí testing ‚Üí release ‚Üí maintenance.
    

* * *

#### 2Ô∏è‚É£ What are the phases of SDLC?

#### 

**Answer:** SDLC has **6 main phases**:

1.  **Requirement Gathering & Analysis:** Understand client needs and document them. üìù
    
2.  **Design:** Plan software architecture, database, and UI/UX. üèóÔ∏è
    
3.  **Implementation / Coding:** Developers write code according to design. üíª
    
4.  **Testing:** Check for bugs, errors, and ensure requirements are met. üêû
    
5.  **Deployment:** Release software to users. üöÄ
    
6.  **Maintenance:** Fix bugs, update software post-launch. üîß
    

*   **Memory Tip:** Remember **‚ÄúR-D-I-T-D-M‚Äù** ‚Üí Requirements, Design, Implementation, Testing, Deployment, Maintenance.

#### 3Ô∏è‚É£ QA vs QC

#### 

**Answer:**

*   **QA (Quality Assurance):** Focuses on **process** to prevent defects. _‚ÄúAre we building the product right?‚Äù_ ‚úÖ
    
*   **QC (Quality Control):** Focuses on **product** to detect defects. _‚ÄúAre we building the right product?‚Äù_ üõ†Ô∏è
    
*   **Example:**
    
    *   QA ‚Üí Defining coding standards & review processes
        
    *   QC ‚Üí Finding bugs in the software during testing
        
*   **Memory Tip:** QA = **Process**, QC = **Product**.

#### 4Ô∏è‚É£ What is the V-Model?

#### 

**Answer:**

*   **Definition:** V-Model is a **Verification & Validation model** where **each development phase has a corresponding testing phase**, forming a V-shape. ‚úÖ
    
*   **Purpose:** Ensures **early testing** and reduces defects later.
    
*   **Example:**
    
    *   Requirements ‚Üî Acceptance Testing
        
    *   Design ‚Üî System Testing
        
    *   Coding ‚Üî Unit Testing
        
*   **Memory Tip:** Think of **V as Verification and Validation** ‚Äî development on the left, testing on the right. üìê
